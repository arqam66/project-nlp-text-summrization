Project Introduction

What is SummAI?

SummAI is a production-ready, interactive text summarization application driven by Natural Language Processing. It aims to reduce readability overhead by condensing large documents into rich, human-quality extracts.

Algorithm Purpose

Computes mathematical importance weights for every sentence by filtering out noise (stopwords) and computing dynamic word-frequency matrices.

Key Significance

Enables instant research synthesis, automatic corporate briefing updates, and facilitates easy reading of long news publications without information overload.

Slide 04

Problem Statement

The Information Overload Challenge

Modern developers, researchers, and students face hundreds of megabytes of daily reading, causing intellectual fatigue and slowing down execution throughput.

Manual Summarization Constraints

Manually distilling articles is slow, highly subjective, prone to personal bias, and impossible to run at large scales (e.g. hundreds of documents at a time).

Why automated NLP is essential

An algorithmic solution is necessary to run consistent, objective, and blindingly fast content evaluations that compress large volumes of unstructured data instantaneously.

Slide 06

Methodology & NLP Pipeline

1
Preprocessing
Text is lowercased and tokenized into constituent words and sentences.
→
2
Noise Filtering
Punctuation & english stopwords (like 'the', 'is', 'at') are filtered out.
→
3
Frequency Matrix
Word frequencies are computed and normalized by maximum occurrences.
→
4
Sentence Scoring
Sentences are scored by summing the normalized frequency weights of its words.
→
5
Ranking & Output
High scoring N-sentences are selected to construct the clean cohesive summary.

Future Enhancements

Abstractive Models

Integrate deep transformer networks like BART, T5, or Pegasus to enable high-quality abstractive summarization (writing completely fresh sentences instead of extracting verbatim lines).

Semantic Embeddings

Introduce cosine similarities utilizing pre-trained Word2Vec or BERT embeddings to improve matches over synonyms and semantic context instead of literal frequency matches.

Enterprise Integrations

Expose the summarizer via secure Dockerized cloud containers to allow seamless API plug-and-play integrations with external enterprise applications and services.
